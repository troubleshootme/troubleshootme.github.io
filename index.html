⚠️ Margin traps most likely to hurt brokers today

  • Underpriced flatbed

    • With +$0.11 paid over posted, flatbed sold off the screen without carrier confirmation is dangerous.

  • Florida inbound sold without exit economics

    • This is still one of the fastest ways to create fake margin.

  • Reefer quoted before capacity is lined up

    • In a seasonal tightening environment, that usually becomes a same-day rebuy.

  • Heavy haul sold before routing is checked

    • Weather, wind restrictions, and permit issues can eat an otherwise good margin immediately.

  • Van assumptions based on national softness

    • A -$0.09 spread helps on balanced lanes, not on ugly geography.

  • Facility-side failures mistaken for carrier failures

    • Storms compress shipping windows.
    • The truck may be ready while the dock is not.
    • Reconfirm shipper and receiver operations before dispatching blame.
