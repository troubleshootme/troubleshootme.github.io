OTRI Trends Tender rejection trends are beginning to diverge. Flatbed rejection rates are expected to climb this week as firming spot rates give carriers more lucrative options. Van rejections will likely remain stable as the market finds equilibrium. The most significant volatility will be in the reefer segment, where rejections are projected to spike in the Southeast and Southwest as carriers reposition to capitalize on the disruption in the tomato supply chain.
