OTRI Trends Tender rejections for reefers are expected to spike, particularly in the Southeast and Southwest, as carriers abandon contract freight for highly lucrative spot market opportunities created by the tomato trade disruption. Van rejections will likely see a modest increase as spot rates firm up. Flatbed rejection rates will remain near historic lows due to widespread equipment availability, forcing carriers to accept tendered loads to maintain utilization.
