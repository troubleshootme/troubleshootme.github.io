The practical interpretation

  • Van:

    • Best use today: regional freight, dense reload lanes, customer freight that can absorb same-day fuel updates.

  • Reefer:

    • Best use today: produce, food, beverage, and any urgent replenishment freight with known cold-chain requirements.
    • Biggest trap: treating reefer like van with a temperature setting. Claims exposure will erase thin margin fast.

  • Flatbed:

    • Best use today: shippers who can provide exact commodity, dimensions, tarp needs, securement rules, and jobsite hours.

  • Heavy Haul / Specialized:

    • Best use today: repeat customers with accurate load data and realistic delivery schedules.

    • Best use today: relationship freight, repeat consolidations, and shippers with disciplined freight descriptions.
    • Biggest trap: cheap partials with changing pallet counts, vague appointments, or fragile freight.
