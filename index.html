  • Higher fuel burn (reefer units working harder)
  • Greater breakdown risk (equipment strain in extreme temps)

🎯 With Cost-Sensitive Shippers

Say the quiet part clearly: Fuel is now a daily input, not a weekly footnote.

  • Fuel surcharge (FSC)

🏭 With Manufacturing / Industrial Accounts

Lead with capacity planning, not rate shopping. Open-deck shippers need to hear:

  • "The truck is the scarce asset."
  • "Spec clarity lowers price more than haggling does."

Ask for commodity, dimensions, securement needs, loading method, unload method, and appointment flexibility upfront.

🥦 With Food / Produce Customers

Position around temperature integrity, not truck availability. In this heat, the cheapest reefer often becomes the most expensive claim. Emphasize pre-cool, reefer fuel, and breakdown response in every conversation.

🛒 With Retail Customers

Offer LTL/partial alternatives where service windows allow—but do not oversell them as magic savings if freight is time-sensitive or cross-dock dependent.
