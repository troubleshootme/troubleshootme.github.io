This creates a high-velocity market where brokers can capitalize on consistent freight volumes. While equipment is generally available, the strong demand prevents significant rate erosion, making it a reliably profitable sector for well-positioned brokers.

OTRI Trends Tender rejections are firming in the van sector as spot rates become more attractive than contract rates, encouraging carriers to play the spot market. Reefer rejection rates remain elevated but stable, particularly in the Southeast, as carriers prioritize lucrative seasonal loads. Flatbed rejections are expected to stay relatively low and stable, as the market's high volume and balanced capacity incentivize carriers to accept tenders to maintain consistent utilization.
