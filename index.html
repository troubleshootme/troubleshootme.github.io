Fuel surcharges remain a critical component of pricing.
OTRI Trends Tender rejections are expected to increase, particularly in the Southeast and Midwest. The combination of extreme heat stressing drivers and equipment, coupled with tightening van capacity, will lead carriers to be more selective. Expect higher rejection rates on loads going into or out of major metropolitan areas affected by the heatwave, necessitating higher rate offers and stronger carrier relationships to ensure load coverage.
