💰 How To Price Today Without Getting Trapped

  • Use a three-part pricing mindset on exposed freight:

    • Linehaul
    • Fuel
    • Disruption premium for detours, dwell, or appointment inflexibility

  • Do not bury all risk into one vague number internally.

    • Even if you sell an all-in price, your desk should know:
    • What portion is normal transport
    • What portion is fuel at $5.533/gallon
    • What portion is weather/schedule exposure

  • Quote-validity discipline matters today.

    • Midwest flood lanes: keep quotes very short
    • Reefer and specialized: quote only after live sourcing when possible
    • Southbound Midwest loads: best pricing window is same-day early

  • Best shipper framing:

    • “We can move this, but today’s price reflects replacement-truck economics, flood-related routing risk, and diesel at $5.533 per gallon.”
    • That language keeps the conversation on service certainty, not just rate resistance.
