This indicates a healthy tension where strong, sustained demand from construction and industrial sectors is keeping rates firm, even with significant capacity in the market. The high volume creates a stable and predictable environment for brokers. The key to profitability is not finding freight, but efficiently matching the constant flow of loads with well-positioned carriers to maximize utilization and margins.
Diesel Price $3.73/gal - The national diesel average has ticked up slightly to $3.73/gallon. Market dynamics, specifically tight capacity and strong demand, remain the dominant factors in rate negotiations.
OTRI Trends Tender rejections are trending upward, particularly for van and reefer equipment. As the spread between spot and contract rates widens, and with paid spot rates climbing, carriers are increasingly rejecting tendered loads in favor of more lucrative spot market opportunities. This is most pronounced in high-demand regions like the Southeast, requiring brokers to anticipate higher-than-normal rejection rates on contract freight and have spot capacity options ready.
