The average paid rate has surged to $2.62/mile, a staggering $0.38 above the average posted rate of $2.24/mile. Even though the week-ago national average was higher at $2.37/mile, the current real-time negotiation leverage is firmly with carriers. The significant positive gap between paid and posted spot rates provides a strong financial incentive for carriers to reject lower-paying contract freight in favor of more lucrative opportunities on the spot market. This trend will be most acute in the heat-affected Southeast and Midwest regions.
