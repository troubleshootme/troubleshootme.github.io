  • Transparency sells: separate FSC from weather/detour adds; customers pay for control, not surprises.

📚 Quick Terminology

  • OTRI (Outbound Tender Rejection Index): % of contracted loads rejected by carriers; higher OTRI implies tighter capacity.
  • FSC (Fuel Surcharge): A per-mile add-on indexed to diesel; keeps base rate comparable over time.
  • OTIF (On Time In Full): Delivery KPI for retail/DC networks; impacts vendor scorecards and penalties.
