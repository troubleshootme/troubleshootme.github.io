    A sharp increase in freight rates for oil transportation, even during its typical off-season, points to volatility in the energy sector. While this directly impacts tanker operations, it has secondary effects for brokers, potentially influencing diesel price futures and increasing demand for flatbed and specialized equipment used in the energy industry.
