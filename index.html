üìä Top-Line Summary

The freight market presents a complex picture of broad economic softness contrasted with acute, regional capacity tightness. Operationally, the focus shifts to the Midwest, where flood watches and extreme heat are creating significant disruption and rate volatility, offering arbitrage opportunities for agile brokers.
