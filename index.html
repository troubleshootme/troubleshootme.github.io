News Insight

Negotiate Fuel Surcharges Separately to Build Carrier Trust

In this extreme fuel environment, quoting an "all-in" rate can create friction. Instead, negotiate the linehaul rate and the fuel surcharge separately. This demonstrates an understanding of the carrier's direct costs and builds trust, often resulting in a more favorable linehaul and a more committed partner. Ensure the surcharge is clearly itemized on the rate confirmation.
