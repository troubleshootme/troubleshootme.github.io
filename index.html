  • Carrier utilization is king: Offer guaranteed same-day/next-day reloads to win the truck at buy rates near posted.
  • Transparency beats opacity in weather: Preempt delay calls with detour maps and revised ETA ranges; shippers pay for control, not surprises.

🔋 Fuel and Cost Guidance

  • Diesel stable at $3.665/gal: Keep FSC constant; avoid raising base linehaul “due to fuel.” If shippers ask, show FSC math and route-driven detour adds as separate line items.
  • Carrier message: “We’ll keep your wheels turning Monday with a pre-booked reload and steady FSC—no games.”

📚 Terminology Quick Hits

  • OTRI (Outbound Tender Rejection Index): % of contracted loads carriers reject; low OTRI = easier capacity.
  • FSC (Fuel Surcharge): A per-mile or % add-on pegged to diesel; keeps base linehaul comparable.
  • OTIF (On Time In Full): Delivery performance metric for retail/DCs impacting vendor scorecards.

✅ Quick Checklist Before You Log Off

  • Book urgent DC/restock vans/reefers first (paid > posted windows).
  • Stage detours and surcharges for any AZ or WY touch.
  • Bundle Monday/Tuesday freight to secure repeat carriers.
  • Send proactive customer updates on any Phoenix/WY impacts with revised ETAs and fee transparency.
  • Hold a 15-minute huddle: Assign two people to watch load boards 5–9 PM local for arbitrage pops.
