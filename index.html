  • Tender-to-award time (target <45 minutes in tight zones).
  • Fall-off rate on reefer/OSOW (target <3%).
  • On-time pickup/delivery in freeze and flood corridors (target >95%).
  • Compliance pass rate (documentation complete at tender; target 100% for CA-linked loads).

🧠 Behavioral Edge

  • Shippers under visible weather risk pay for certainty. Lead with SOPs and time-stamped alerts; offer small bundles with rate holds.
  • Carriers will trade pennies for guaranteed reloads. Publish exact reloads and appointment windows at time of tender; award fast and reduce haggle.
