üìä Top-Line Summary

The national freight market is defined by increasing load volumes across all major equipment types, signaling robust demand. However, rate dynamics are diverging. Van rates are showing upward pressure with paid rates surpassing posted averages, indicating a tightening market for reliable capacity. The reefer market remains stressed by widespread, severe heatwaves across the southern U.S., creating equipment strain and capacity challenges. The Southeast is a critical focal point, experiencing a convergence of extreme heat, severe thunderstorms, and high freight volume, creating significant operational challenges and margin opportunities for agile brokers.
